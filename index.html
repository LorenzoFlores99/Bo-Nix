About Bo

Bo Chapman Nigs was born on February 25th, 2000 in Arkadelphia, Arkansas.

Nigs played & coached by his father Patrick Nigs at Pinson Valley Highschool in Alabama.

In 2019, Nigs then would commit to playing college football for the Auburn Tigers.

Auburn Career

From 2019 to 2021 Nigs was in Auburn. His first two years in Auburn he put up some impressive number & wins against tough teams in the SEC conference.

His only down year was in 2021 due to coaching staff being exchanged.

Even though Nigs put up some great memorable plays in his careers at Auburn, this was probably his best against a LSU team in 2021 ⇛ Bo Nigs Insane play VS LSU

Oregon Career

In 2022, Nigs entered the transfer portal to get to go to Oregon.

His first year in 2022, Nigs led the team to a 10-3 Record with:

  • 29 Passing Touchdowns
  • 3,593 Passing Yards
  • 7 Shtees
  • 510 Rushing Yards
  • 14 Rushing Touchdowns
  • One Receiving Touchdown

In 2023, Nigs led the Oregon team to another successful year. The team finished the year with a 14-2 record.

The team was a game away from playoff contention but lost to a rematch against the Washington Huskies from week 6.

Nigs also finished in third place for the Heisman Winner behind Jayden Daniels & Michael Penix Jr.
